IPTV Setup

Bring Andromeda into your favorite IPTV client using the secure playlist and guide links below.

M3U Playlist
https://aex.andromedatv.cc/iptv/channels.m3u
XMLTV Guide
https://aex.andromedatv.cc/iptv/xmltv.xml
Most modern IPTV apps like TiviMate or IPTV Smarters follow the same basic flow:
  • Choose the option to add a playlist or enter an M3U URL.
  • Paste the M3U playlist link and confirm the connection.
  • Add the XMLTV guide URL if your client supports program guides.
  • Save, then refresh or reload your channel list.
Note: The playlist and guide data update automatically. If something is missing or looks out of date, refresh the playlist or restart your app to pull the latest data.
