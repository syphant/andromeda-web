IPTV Setup

Connection URLs

M3U:
https://aex.andromedatv.cc/iptv/channels.m3u
XMLTV:
https://aex.andromedatv.cc/iptv/xmltv.xml

Setup Instructions

For most IPTV clients (TiviMate, IPTV Smarters, etc):

  • Look for "Add Playlist" or "M3U URL" option
  • Enter the M3U URL provided above
  • If your client supports EPG (Electronic Program Guide), add the XMLTV URL
  • Save and refresh your channel list

Note: The channel list and program guide are updated automatically. If you experience any issues, try refreshing your playlist or restarting your IPTV client.
